The ISB Software Development Club was established in 2014 to provide ISB students the means to put their talents, in the field of computer science, to work. The club's success results from the passion to self-educate and the determination that the students have to independently perform tasks in a timely manner. The Software Development Club's primary objective is to use the perseverance and creativity of programmers at ISB to serve the community. A gallery of the club's past contributions to ISB, through applications and web development, can be viewed on the Portfolio page. A list of the club's members can be viewed on the Members page.

Current Project: ISB ACE


     ISB Ace is the first of many projects to be developed by the ISB Software Development club. This application features a variety of useful tools to assist the every-day ISB student! These features include:

Your current grades
  • Notifications when your grades change
  • A grade calculator
  • Your schedule
  • A news feed with the laters Panther Productions broadcasts and International articles

     Currently this application is only available for Android users, however the Software Development Club aims to release an iOS and Mac OSX version this year as well.
